  • $H_0$: Average Weight of Men = Average Weight of Women | Avg_Wt(Men) - Avg_Wt(W) = 0

Step 02: Define the Level of Significance or Alpha¶

  • Alpha = 0.05 (T Table) and the Sample Size is < 30 and hence we will use T-Test => Two Sample T-Test

Anova¶

About F Distribution¶

To perform an Anova Test, we need to compare two kinds of variation, variation between sample means & the variation within each of the sample means.

We combine all of this into a single statistic and that statistic is called F Statistic.

How to Calculate Anova:: Steps¶

  1. Find out the Degrees of Freedom for Within & Between
  2. DFn - Degrees of Freedom Between (k-1)
  3. DFd - Degrees of Freedom Within (n-k)
  4. Find the Mean of all the Columns & Grand Mean (Mean of all the Data Points)
  5. Find the Sum of Squares Total(SST): Sum of Difference of Data Points & Grand Mean
  6. Find the Sum of Squares Within(SSW): Sum of Difference of Data Points & resp Sample Means.
  7. Sum of Squares Between = SST - SSW
  8. Mean Squares Between = SSB/Dfn
  9. Mean Square Within = SSW/DFd
  10. Find the Critical Value and F Test Statistic (FStats = MSb/MSw),Compare and Conclude.

Chi Square¶

  • In a bank atm, a manager wants to know if the no of people visiting atm is Same for all the Days.

  • If the Food Offered in Any Restaurant is in sync with the customer's preference.

  • Categories vs Categories | No Numbers | No Numerical Data in form of Revenue, Cost etc...

  • The Data involved in Analysis is Frequency Data (value_counts)

  • A Chi Square Test is a test of Hypothesis where two samples (categories) are tested for independence.

  • It means if samples are independent, there is no effect.

  • For e.g. in titanic, Passenger Class has an impact on Survival.

  • Chi Square is a Non Paramteric Test. A NP Test is a test which does not have any assumptions about the data.

  • Chi Square Test is based on differences between Observed Values and Expected Values sum[(Obs-Exp)^2/Exp]

def chisquares(data):
    teststats, pvalue, df, exp_freq = stats.chi2_contingency(data)

    print("PValue is ", pvalue)
    if(pvalue<0.05):
        return("Reject Ho")
    else:
        return("Fail to Reject Ho")
